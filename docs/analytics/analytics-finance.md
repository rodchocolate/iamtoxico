# Analytics and Finance

Analytics
- GA4: gtag install, purchase events.
- Meta Pixel: PageView, ViewContent, AddToCart, InitiateCheckout, Purchase.
- Post-purchase survey (source of awareness).

Finance
- COGS sheet (Printify per variant) + shipping + fees.
- Price ladder and discounts.
- KPI targets: CTR, CVR, AOV, CAC, ROAS, gross margin.

## Underwear capsule
- Metrics: category CVR vs apparel baseline, refund rate (hygiene), repeat purchase rate.
- Pricing: map COGS (elastic customization) + shipping + margin; consider bundle (2-pack) pricing.
- Experiments: A/B waistband cursive vs plain; PDP video vs static.
