# Underwear Capsule — Design Spec (Aug 2025 – Jan 2026)

Products
- Women’s thong
- Men’s boxers

Branding
- Waistband slogans:
  - Women: "i love toxico"
  - Men: "i am toxico"
- Back print both: "handle with care"
- Typography: lowercase, consider subtle cursive for waistband only; back print in small caps sans.

Color plan
- 6 months, 2 clashing colors per month = 12 SKUs.
- Example (Aug): Thong — Neon Pink + white waistband; Boxers — Lime Green + white waistband.
- Finalize 5 more month pairings; ensure contrast and legibility.

File production
- Deliverables: print-ready PNGs + vector waistband repeats.
- Canvas: follow supplier artboard for elastic repeats; maintain sRGB profile.
- Naming: underwear_<month>_<style>_<color>_waistband-<slogan>.png

Mockups
- Include front/back; show waistband detail.

QC
- Check text baseline alignment in repeat; no seam cutoff.
