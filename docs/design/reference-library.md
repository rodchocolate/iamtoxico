# Reference Library — Public Domain / CC0 (Well‑dressed Black/Brown men)

Purpose
- Curate license‑safe vintage imagery for brand mood, editorial, and potential collateral.

Usage guidelines
- Prefer Public Domain (PD) or CC0 for commercial use. CC‑BY requires attribution (add to site footer/press page).
- Check rights statements on each item page. Avoid images with restrictions.
- Recognizable people: for ads/packaging, consider publicity/privacy rights; safer as editorial/blog/mood.

Primary sources (start here)
- Library of Congress (PD)
  - William P. Gottlieb Jazz Photos — elegant suits, musicians.
  - Harris & Ewing Collection — D.C. studio/press portraits.
  - Bain News Service — early 1900s news/portraits.
  - FSA/OWI B&W negatives — Sunday best, community life.
  - Search terms: “Harlem”, “zoot suit”, “gentleman”, “Latin American”, “Cuban”, “Mexican”.
- Smithsonian Open Access (CC0)
  - Filter: CC0. Queries: “portrait man suit”, “Harlem”, “jazz”.
- NYPL Digital Collections (Public Domain)
  - Schomburg Center materials. Queries: “Harlem dandies”, “zoot suit”, “street style”.
- National Archives (US) (PD)
  - Queries: “African American soldiers dress uniform”, “band portrait”.
- The Met — Open Access (CC0)
  - Historic studio portraits; filter to Open Access.
- Digital Public Library of America (DPLA)
  - Aggregated search; filter to “No known copyright”/CC0.

Additional regional sources
- Calisphere (UC) — check rights per item. Queries: “Los Angeles zoot suit”, “Oakland 1940s”.
- Portal to Texas History (UNT) — many PD. Queries: “Mexican American social club”, “El Paso men suit”.
- dLOC (Digital Library of the Caribbean) — rights vary. Queries: “Havana gentlemen”, “Trinidad portrait”.
- Flickr Commons — institution PD uploads (British Library, National Library of Jamaica, etc.). Filter “No known copyright”.

Curation checklist
- Resolution ≥ 2000px on long edge (prefer TIFF/JPEG masters).
- Attire: suits, dress shirts, ties, hats; strong posture/gesture.
- Clean background or crop‑able composition.
- Rights: PD/CC0/No known restrictions; download original; save rights text.
- Metadata: title, creator, date, source URL.
