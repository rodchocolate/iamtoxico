## üñ®Ô∏è PHASE 4: FULFILLMENT STRATEGY

### Print-on-Demand (POD) Options
| Service | Pros | Cons |
|---------|------|------|
| **Printful** | US/EU fulfillment, quality | Higher cost |
| **Printify** | Multiple print providers | Variable quality |
| **Gooten** | Good international | Less control |
| **SPOD** | Fast US shipping | Limited selection |

### Low Stock In-House
- Start with 50-100 units of bestsellers
- Use 3PL (ShipBob, Deliverr) once volume justifies
- Keep POD as backup/overflow

### Hybrid Model
1. **POD** for long-tail SKUs, new designs
2. **In-house** for core bestsellers (better margin, faster shipping)
3. **Affiliate** for aspirational/reference products
