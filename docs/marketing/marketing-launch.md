# Marketing Launch Plan

Objectives
- Capture emails prelaunch, drive first 50 orders in week 1.

Channels
- Instagram/TikTok, email, site teaser.

Plan (2 weeks)
- T-14 to T-7: teaser loops (macro crop of design), email waitlist.
- T-6 to T-2: mockups, behind-the-scenes, sizing info, reminder.
- T-1: drop time announced, countdown.
- T: launch reel, link in bio, email blast.
- T+1 to T+7: UGC reposts, restock note, "How it fits" content.

Assets
- 6 short verticals, 6 stills, 1 hero banner, PDP video loop.

Influencer seeding
- 10 micro creators; DM script + form; ship samples.

Email
- Welcome flow (2 emails), launch, follow-up with reviews.

## Underwear capsule
- Content: 6-month clashing color story; monthly teaser + reveal (front/back), waistband detail macro.
- Copy anchors: “i love toxico” (women), “i am toxico” (men), “handle with care” (back).
- Calendar: align with monthly color drops; collect waitlist per month.
- UGC: fit checks and comfort reviews; creator seeding in August colors first.
