- **Brand Architecture**: Lowercase "iamtoxico" naming convention with edgy, minimal aesthetic
- **Visual Identity**: High-quality artwork assets prepared for print production

## Market Positioning

### Target Demographics
- **Primary Market**: 18-35 year olds interested in edgy, casual fashion
- **Secondary Market**: Fashion-forward consumers seeking unique loungewear
- **Geographic Focus**: English-speaking markets with expansion potential
- **Psychographics**: Urban, digitally-native, brand-conscious consumers
- **Price Sensitivity**: Mid-range pricing with premium brand positioning

### Competitive Advantage
- **Distinctive Branding**: Memorable name and provocative messaging
- **Quality Materials**: Premium fabric selection and printing techniques
- **Limited Releases**: Scarcity marketing through monthly color rotations

- **Product Sales**: Primary revenue from direct-to-consumer sales
